Privacy Policy

Budget Budy

Budget Budy ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Budget Budy mobile application.

Information We Collect

Budget Budy does not require personal identification information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Expense Data

The app allows you to:

  • Add expenses
  • View daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly expenses
  • Sort spendings by date (recent first)
  • Delete spendings
  • View analytics of your daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly spendings

All expense data you enter is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

How We Use Your Information

  • Display your expense records
  • Generate spending summaries
  • Provide spending analytics and insights
  • Allow sorting and deletion of expenses

Data Storage & Security

Your data remains on your device. We do not upload, share, or back up your expense data to any server. Reasonable security measures are used to protect your data, but no digital storage method is 100% secure.

Data Sharing

Budget Budy does not sell, trade, rent, or share your data with any third party.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may delete individual expenses at any time. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all stored data from your device.

Children’s Privacy

Budget Budy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
